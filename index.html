Time Left Until the Event:

Message from the Secretary General

Dear Delegates, Advisors, and Distinguished Participants,

As the Secretary-General of BUTECHMUN’25, I am thrilled to invite you to our first-ever conference, which will take place on May 16–17, 2025, at Bursa Technical University’s Mimar Sinan Campus.

We are proud to open our doors to a new chapter of diplomacy and dialogue through the inaugural edition of BUTECHMUN! BUTECHMUN’25 is the first MUN conference at the university level in Bursa.

Our dedicated and passionate team has been working tirelessly to bring Bursa Technical University Model United Nations 2025 to life in a truly one-of-a-kind way — an event that will leave lasting memories for every participant.

This year, the overarching theme of our conference is Sustainability. With this in mind, our primary aim is to foster high-level diplomatic debates and passionate discussions focused on pressing environmental issues of global importance. We believe that by working together, delegates can develop innovative, impactful, and actionable solutions that contribute to a better future for our planet and for billions of lives around the world.

Model UN is more than just what happens in committee rooms — it's a space to learn, connect, and be inspired. Take this opportunity to enhance your skills, meet remarkable individuals, and make memories that will last a lifetime. In May 16-17, 2025, I hope you embrace every opportunity to grow, engage, and enjoy the journey.

Until we meet in May,

AYŞENUR BUSE KARAMAN
SECRETARY-GENERAL of BUTECHMUN’25

Message from the Directory General

Dear Esteemed Participants,

It is with immense pride and excitement that I welcome you to the very first edition of BUTECHMUN’25—a conference where ideas will ignite, voices will rise, and history will be made.


As the Director General, I, Bilge Akbaş, am beyond honored to lead this groundbreaking event. BUTECHMUN’25 is not just our university’s first MUN conference; it is the beginning of a legacy that we are all writing together. We are making history—and we’re thrilled to have you as part of it.


This year, our focus is on sustainability—an issue that demands bold thinking, global cooperation, and urgent action. Through dynamic debates and meaningful dialogue, we aim to empower delegates to challenge the status quo and shape a better, greener future.

Our team has worked with unwavering passion to create a platform where your voice matters. Whether you're a seasoned delegate or taking your first steps into MUN, we invite you to speak up, collaborate deeply, and make your mark.

Get ready for a journey full of diplomacy, inspiration, and unforgettable memories. The future starts here, and it starts with us.



Warmest regards,

Bilge Akbaş
DIRECTORY-GENERAL of BUTECHMUN’25
